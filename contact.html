Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What are the school hours?

Regular school hours are from 7:30 AM to 2:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Extra-curricular activities may extend until 4:30 PM.

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our website, visit our admissions office, or contact us via WhatsApp. Required documents include birth certificate, previous school reports, and passport photos.

What is the fee structure?

Fees vary by grade level. Please contact our admissions office for the current fee structure, payment plans, and available scholarships.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we offer school bus services covering major routes. Contact our transport office for routes, schedules, and fees.
